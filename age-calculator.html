What is Age Calculator?

An Age Calculator helps you find your exact age in years, months and days. It also shows total days lived, next birthday countdown and age in months, weeks, hours.

Example: If DOB is 15-Aug-1995 and today is 04-Apr-2026, Your Age = 30 Years 7 Months 20 Days. Total Days Lived = 11,195 Days. Next Birthday in 133 Days.

Why Use Age Calculator?

1

Official Use: School admission, Job application, Passport, PAN, Aadhar age proof.

2

Birthday Reminder: Know exactly how many days left for next birthday.

3

Retirement Planning: Calculate years left for retirement at age 60.

4

Insurance & Loans: Most policies and loans have age eligibility criteria.

Key Insight: Age is calculated from DOB to current date. Leap years are automatically adjusted.

Disclaimer: Age calculated based on Gregorian calendar. For legal documents always refer to birth certificate.
