What is Public Provident Fund PPF?

PPF is a government backed long term savings scheme. Lock-in period is 15 years. Investment up to ₹1.5 Lakh per year is tax free under 80C. Interest earned and maturity amount both are tax free. Current PPF rate is 7.1% compounded yearly.

PPF Example: If you invest ₹1,50,000 every year for 15 years at 7.1%, your total investment = ₹22,50,000. Maturity amount = ₹40,68,209. Tax free gain = ₹18,18,209.

Why Choose PPF Investment?

1

Tax Free Returns: EEE benefit. Investment, Interest and Maturity all tax free.

2

Govt Guaranteed: 100% safe. No market risk. Backed by Govt of India.

3

Long Term Wealth: 15 year compounding creates huge corpus for retirement.

4

Loan & Withdrawal: Loan available from 3rd year. Partial withdrawal from 7th year.

Key Insight: Max investment ₹1.5 Lakh per year. You can extend PPF in blocks of 5 years after 15 years. Best for retirement planning.

Disclaimer: PPF interest rate is decided by Govt every quarter. Current rate 7.1% for Q1 2026. This calculator is for illustration only. Max investment limit ₹1.5 Lakh per financial year.
