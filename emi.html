What is EMI?

EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) is a fixed amount paid every month to repay a loan. Each EMI includes both the principal amount and the interest charged by the lender.

Easy Monthly Payments

Repay your loan in small monthly installments instead of making one large payment.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is EMI?

EMI is the fixed monthly amount you pay to repay a loan.

Can I prepay my loan?

Yes. Many lenders allow part-prepayment or full prepayment, though charges may apply depending on the loan terms.

Does a longer tenure reduce EMI?

Yes. A longer tenure generally lowers the monthly EMI but increases the total interest paid.

Can I change my EMI amount later?

It depends on your lender. Some lenders allow restructuring or refinancing under certain conditions.
