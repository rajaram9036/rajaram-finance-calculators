❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is EMI?

EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) is the fixed monthly amount paid to repay a loan.

How is EMI calculated?

EMI depends on the loan amount, annual interest rate and loan tenure.

Can I reduce my EMI?

Yes. You can reduce EMI by increasing the loan tenure, making a larger down payment, or getting a lower interest rate.

Is prepayment beneficial?

Prepaying your loan can reduce both the outstanding principal and the total interest paid over the loan period.
