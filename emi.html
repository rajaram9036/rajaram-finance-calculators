What is EMI Calculator?

EMI = Equated Monthly Installment. This calculator helps you find monthly payment for any loan - Home Loan, Car Loan, Personal Loan. EMI includes both Principal + Interest. Longer tenure = Lower EMI but Higher Interest.

EMI Example: For ₹10,00,000 loan at 10% for 10 years, your EMI = ₹13,215. Total Interest = ₹5,85,809. Total Payment = ₹15,85,809.

How to Reduce Your EMI Burden?

1

Increase Down Payment: Higher down payment means lower loan amount and lower EMI.

2

Prepayment: Pay extra whenever possible to reduce principal and interest.

3

Balance Transfer: Transfer loan to bank with lower interest rate.

4

Shorter Tenure: Choose shorter tenure to save lakhs in interest.

Key Insight: Always check processing fees and other charges before taking loan. Compare 3-4 banks before finalizing.

Disclaimer: EMI shown here is for illustration only. Actual EMI may vary based on bank, processing fees, and interest calculation method. Consult your bank for exact figures.
