What is Recurring Deposit RD?

A Recurring Deposit or RD is like FD but you invest a small amount every month. It's perfect for salaried people who want to save regularly. Interest is compounded quarterly and paid at maturity. Tenure ranges from 6 months to 10 years.

RD Example: If you invest ₹5,000 per month for 5 years at 7.5% interest, your maturity amount will be ₹3,57,695. Total investment = ₹3,00,000. Interest = ₹57,695.

Why Choose RD Investment?

1

Disciplined Saving: Builds saving habit by investing fixed amount every month.

2

Guaranteed Returns: Like FD, RD also gives fixed and guaranteed returns. No market risk.

3

Low Entry Amount: Start RD with just ₹500 per month. Good for beginners.

4

Flexible Tenure: Choose from 6 months to 10 years based on your goal.

Key Insight: RD is best for short to medium term goals like vacation, gadget, downpayment. Interest is taxable.

Disclaimer: RD rates vary from bank to bank. Interest earned is taxable. Penalty for missed installments. This calculator is for illustration only.
