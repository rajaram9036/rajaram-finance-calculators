What is SIP Calculator and How Does It Work?

A Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) Calculator is a financial tool that helps investors estimate the future value of their mutual fund investments. By entering monthly investment amount, expected return rate, and tenure, you get instant projections of wealth creation.

SIP works on the principle of rupee cost averaging and power of compounding. You invest a fixed amount regularly, which buys more units when markets are low and fewer when markets are high. Over long term, this averages out and reduces risk.

Our Rajaram Finance SIP Calculator uses standard AMFI formula to provide accurate estimates. This tool is ideal for retirement planning, children's education, and wealth building goals.
