❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is SIP?

SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) is a disciplined way to invest a fixed amount regularly in mutual funds.

Can I start SIP with ₹500?

Yes. Many mutual funds allow you to start investing with just ₹500 per month.

Is SIP Risk Free?

No. Mutual Fund investments are subject to market risks. SIP helps reduce the impact of market volatility over time.

Can I stop SIP anytime?

Yes. You can pause or stop your SIP according to your investment platform's rules.
