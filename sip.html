📘 SIP Formula

M = P × (((1+i)n − 1) / i) × (1+i)

M = Maturity Amount
P = Monthly Investment
i = Monthly Interest Rate
n = Total Number of Months

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is SIP?

SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) allows you to invest a fixed amount regularly in mutual funds.

Can I start SIP with ₹500?

Yes. Many mutual funds allow SIP investments starting from ₹500 per month.

Is SIP risk-free?

No. Mutual fund investments are subject to market risks, but SIP helps invest regularly over time.
