Quick Actions

What is SIP?

A Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) allows you to invest a fixed amount regularly in mutual funds. It promotes disciplined investing and can help build wealth over the long term through the power of compounding.

Power of Compounding

Returns earned over time may generate additional returns, helping investments grow over the long term.

Rupee Cost Averaging

Regular investments across different market conditions may reduce the impact of price fluctuations.

Disciplined Investing

Investing a fixed amount every month helps develop a consistent investment habit.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is SIP?

SIP is a method of investing a fixed amount regularly in mutual funds.

What is the minimum SIP amount?

Many mutual funds allow SIPs starting from ₹100 or ₹500, depending on the scheme.

Can I stop or pause my SIP?

Yes. Most platforms allow you to pause or stop future SIP installments according to their process.

Does SIP guarantee returns?

No. Mutual fund returns are market-linked and are not guaranteed.
