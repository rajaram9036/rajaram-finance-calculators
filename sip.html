Frequently Asked Questions

What is SIP?

SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) allows you to invest a fixed amount every month in Mutual Funds.

Can I start SIP with ₹500?

Yes. Most Mutual Funds allow SIP from ₹500 per month.

Is SIP Safe?

Mutual Fund investments are subject to market risks. SIP helps average investment cost over time.
