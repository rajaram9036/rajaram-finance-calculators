What is SIP?

A Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) is a simple way to invest a fixed amount regularly in mutual funds. It encourages disciplined investing and helps build wealth over the long term through the power of compounding.

Power of Compounding

Small investments made consistently can grow significantly over time because returns may also generate returns.

Rupee Cost Averaging

Regular investing across different market conditions can reduce the impact of market volatility over time.

Disciplined Investing

A fixed monthly investment habit can help you stay focused on your long-term financial goals.
