❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common SIP investment questions.

What is SIP?

SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) allows you to invest a fixed amount regularly in mutual funds, helping build wealth over time through disciplined investing.

What is the minimum SIP amount?

Many mutual funds allow SIPs starting from ₹100 or ₹500, depending on the scheme.

Can I stop my SIP anytime?

Yes. You can pause or stop your SIP according to the rules of your mutual fund platform.

Is SIP risk free?

No. SIP invests in mutual funds, whose value depends on market performance. Returns are not guaranteed.

Does SIP help in long-term wealth creation?

Regular investing over long periods may benefit from compounding and disciplined investing.
