What is SIP? Systematic Investment Plan

SIP ka matlab hai Systematic Investment Plan. Isme aap har mahine chota amount mutual fund me invest karke badi wealth bana sakte hain. Power of compounding ki wajah se ₹1000 mahine bhi 10 saal me ₹2.3 Lakh ban sakte hain.

Jitni jaldi shuru karenge utna fayda. Long term me SIP FD se behtar return deta hai.
