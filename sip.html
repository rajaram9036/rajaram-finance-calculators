SIP Calculator kya hai?

Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) mutual fund me har mahine fixed amount invest karne ka tarika hai. Ye chote amount se badi wealth banane me help karta hai. Hamara calculator compounding ke sath step-up SIP bhi calculate karta hai. Agar aap 10 saal tak ₹10000 mahine 12% par invest karte hain to aap ₹23 Lakh se zyada bana sakte hain. Jitni jaldi shuru karenge utna fayda hoga kyunki compounding time ke sath badhta hai. Ye tool 100% free hai aur koi data save nahi karta.
