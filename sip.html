❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is SIP?

SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) lets you invest a fixed amount regularly in mutual funds.

Can I start SIP with ₹500?

Yes. Many mutual funds allow SIP investments starting from ₹500.

Can I increase my SIP later?

Yes. You can increase your SIP amount anytime based on your financial goals.

Is SIP safe?

Mutual Fund investments are subject to market risks. SIP helps reduce volatility over long investment periods.
