SIP Calculator kya hai? - ET Money Style

Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) mutual fund me invest karne ka sabse popular tarika hai. Isme aap har mahine ek fixed amount invest karte hain. Jaise RD hota hai, waise hi SIP hai, lekin returns market par depend karte hain.

Hamara Rajaram Finance SIP Calculator aapko batata hai ki agar aap ₹10000 mahine 12% return par 10 saal tak invest karte hain to aapko kitna milega. Calculator compounding ka use karta hai, isliye chota amount bhi badi wealth bana deta hai.

Step-Up SIP ka feature sabse khaas hai. Agar aapki salary har saal 10% badhti hai to aap SIP bhi 10% bada sakte hain. Isse aap 10 saal me 2x zyada paisa bana sakte hain. Ye calculator wahi calculation karta hai.

Power of Compounding: Pehle 5 saal me aapka paisa dheere badhega, lekin agle 5 saal me bahut tez. Isliye jaldi shuru karna zaroori hai. ₹5000 mahine 20 saal tak 12% par lagao to ₹50 Lakh se zyada ban jata hai.

FD vs SIP: FD me 6-7% fixed milta hai aur safe hai. SIP me risk hai lekin 12-15% long term return mil sakta hai. Agar aapka goal 5 saal se zyada ka hai to SIP behtar option hai.

Is calculator ko use karke aap apne ghar, shaadi, baccho ki padhai, retirement ke liye planning kar sakte hain. PDF download karke apne financial advisor ko bhi dikha sakte hain. Investment shuru karne se pehle ek baar expert se salah zaroor lein.
