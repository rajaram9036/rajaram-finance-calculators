Frequently Asked Questions

What is SIP?

SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) allows you to invest a fixed amount every month in Mutual Funds.

Can I start SIP with ₹500?

Yes. Many Mutual Funds allow SIP investments starting from ₹500.

What is Step-Up SIP?

Step-Up SIP lets you increase your SIP amount every year to build a larger investment corpus over time.
