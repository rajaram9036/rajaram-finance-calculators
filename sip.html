4 Calculation Formula

FV = P × ((1 + r)n − 1) / r × (1 + r)

P = Monthly Investment

r = Monthly Return Rate (Expected Annual Return ÷ 12 ÷ 100)

n = Total Number of Monthly Installments

This formula estimates the future value of a monthly SIP based on your selected inputs.

5 Complete Guide About SIP

What is SIP?

SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) is a simple way to invest a fixed amount regularly in mutual funds.

It helps build wealth over the long term through disciplined investing and the power of compounding.

How SIP Works?

  • ✅ Choose a Mutual Fund
  • ✅ Decide Monthly Amount
  • ✅ Select SIP Date
  • ✅ Invest Every Month
  • ✅ Stay Invested for Long Term
  • ✅ Grow Wealth through Compounding

Benefits of SIP

  • ✔ Rupee Cost Averaging
  • ✔ Power of Compounding
  • ✔ Disciplined Investing
  • ✔ Affordable Investing
  • ✔ Long-term Wealth Creation
  • ✔ Flexible Investment Amount

FAQs

What is the minimum SIP amount?

Many mutual funds allow SIPs starting from ₹100 or ₹500.

Can I stop SIP anytime?

Yes. You can pause or stop SIP according to the mutual fund platform rules.

How is SIP return calculated?

Returns are estimated using the expected annual return entered in the calculator.
