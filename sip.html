Complete Guide About SIP

🌱 What is SIP?

SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) is a simple way to invest a fixed amount every month in Mutual Funds. It helps build wealth through disciplined investing and the power of compounding.

📘 How SIP Works?

  • ✅ Choose Mutual Fund
  • ✅ Set Monthly Amount
  • ✅ Select SIP Date
  • ✅ Auto Debit Every Month
  • ✅ Stay Invested
  • ✅ Grow Wealth with Compounding

📈 Benefits of SIP

  • 💹 Power of Compounding
  • 💰 Rupee Cost Averaging
  • 🎯 Goal Based Investment
  • 🛡 Disciplined Saving
  • 📉 Lower Market Timing Risk
  • 🚀 Long-Term Wealth Creation

🎯 Example

Monthly SIP: ₹5,000

Duration: 20 Years

Expected Return: 12%

Estimated Value: ₹49,95,741

❓ FAQs

What is the minimum SIP amount?

Most Mutual Funds allow SIP from ₹100 or ₹500.

Can I stop SIP anytime?

Yes. You can stop, pause or modify your SIP whenever required.

Is SIP safe?

SIP invests in Mutual Funds, so returns depend on market performance.

Who should invest through SIP?

Students, salaried employees, business owners and long-term investors.
