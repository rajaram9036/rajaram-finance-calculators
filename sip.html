What is SIP? Systematic Investment Plan Guide

SIP ka matlab hai Systematic Investment Plan. Isme aap har mahine chota amount mutual fund me invest karke badi wealth bana sakte hain. Power of compounding ki wajah se ₹5000 mahine bhi 20 saal me ₹50 Lakh ban sakte hain. Hamara calculator aapko exact maturity value batata hai.

Step-Up SIP sabse best feature hai. Agar aap har saal apna SIP 10% badhate hain to aap 2x zyada return paa sakte hain. Ye calculator step-up ko bhi support karta hai.
