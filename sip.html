❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is SIP?

SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) is a simple way to invest a fixed amount regularly in mutual funds.

Can I start SIP with ₹500?

Yes. Most mutual funds allow SIP investments starting from ₹500 per month.

Is SIP better than FD?

SIP and FD serve different purposes. SIP offers market-linked growth, while FD provides fixed returns.

Can I stop SIP anytime?

Yes. You can pause or stop your SIP according to your investment platform's rules.
