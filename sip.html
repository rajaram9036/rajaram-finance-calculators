Frequently Asked Questions

What is SIP?

SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) allows you to invest a fixed amount regularly in Mutual Funds.

Can I start SIP with ₹500?

Yes, many Mutual Funds allow SIP from ₹500 per month.

Can I stop SIP anytime?

Yes. You can stop or modify your SIP as per your investment platform.
