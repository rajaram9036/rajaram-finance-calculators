Formula Used

SIP Formula: M = P × [{(1 + r)^n - 1} / r] × (1 + r)

Where M = Maturity Value, P = Monthly Investment, r = Monthly Rate, n = Total Months. For Step-up SIP, each year investment increases by given %.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is SIP?

SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) allows you to invest a fixed amount every month in Mutual Funds and build wealth over time.

Can I start SIP with ₹500?

Yes. Most Mutual Funds in India allow SIP investments starting from ₹500 per month.

What is Step-Up SIP?

Step-Up SIP lets you increase your SIP amount every year. It helps you invest more as your income grows.

Is SIP better than FD?

SIP in equity mutual funds can give higher long term returns than FD, but it comes with market risk.

Can I stop SIP anytime?

Yes. You can pause or stop your SIP anytime without any penalty.

What is SIP Calculator?
