What is Lumpsum Investment?

Lumpsum Investment means investing a big amount at one time. Formula: A = P(1 + r/100)^t. Best for Mutual Funds, FD, Bonds, PPF when you have idle cash.

Lumpsum Example: Invest ₹1,00,000 at 12% for 10 years. Maturity Amount = ₹3,10,585. Total Gains = ₹2,10,585. Your money becomes 3x in 10 years.

5 Benefits of Lumpsum Investing

1

Power of Compounding: One-time investment grows exponentially over long term.

2

Best for Windfall: Bonus, inheritance, sale of property - invest all at once.

3

Higher Returns: Equity Mutual Funds give 12-15% in long term vs FD 6-7%.

4

No Market Timing: Invest when you have money, let compounding do the work.

5

Tax Efficient: Equity LTCG tax only 10% above ₹1 Lakh after 1 year.

Key Insight: Lumpsum works best for 5+ years. For short term use FD or Debt Funds. For long term use Equity Mutual Funds.
