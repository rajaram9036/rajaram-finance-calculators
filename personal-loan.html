Personal Loan Breakdown

What is Personal Loan EMI Calculator?

A Personal Loan is an unsecured loan for any purpose - medical, wedding, travel, education. No collateral needed. Interest rate is higher 10.5% to 18%. Tenure 1 to 5 years. Approval in 24-48 hours.

Personal Loan Example: For ₹5,00,000 loan at 14% for 3 years, your EMI = ₹17,075. Total Interest = ₹1,14,700. Total Payment = ₹6,14,700.

5 Tips Before Taking Personal Loan

1

Check CIBIL Score: Score above 750 gets lowest rate 10.5% - 12%. Below 700 rate goes to 16-18%.

2

Compare Processing Fee: Banks charge 1% to 2.5% processing fee + GST. This adds to cost.

3

Shorter Tenure: Take 2-3 year tenure. 5 year EMI is low but interest is double.

4

Prepayment Charges: Check prepayment penalty. Some banks charge 3-5% if you close early.

5

Borrow Only What You Need: Personal loan interest is high. Don't borrow extra money.

Key Insight: Personal loan is costliest loan. Use only for emergency. For big expenses prefer Home Loan or Loan against FD.
