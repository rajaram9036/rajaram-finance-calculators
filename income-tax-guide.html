What is Income Tax?

Income Tax is a tax levied by the Government of India on the income earned by individuals and certain entities according to the applicable tax laws. The amount of tax payable depends on factors such as taxable income, the chosen tax regime and the applicable provisions of the Income-tax Act.

  • ✅ Encourages timely tax compliance.

How Does Income Tax Work?

Income tax is calculated on taxable income according to the applicable tax slabs and rules. Tax laws, rates, deductions and exemptions may change over time, so it is important to refer to the latest official information.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Who is required to pay Income Tax?

Income tax liability depends on your taxable income, applicable tax laws and other eligibility conditions. Please refer to the latest official rules for your situation.

2. What is the difference between the Old and New Tax Regime?

The two tax regimes have different tax rates and rules regarding deductions and exemptions. The most suitable option depends on your individual financial circumstances and the applicable tax provisions.

3. Can tax rules change?

Yes. Tax slabs, deductions, exemptions and other provisions may change through updates in tax laws and government announcements.

4. Does this calculator provide the exact tax amount?

No. The calculator provides an estimated value for educational purposes. Your actual tax liability may differ based on the latest laws and your individual circumstances.
