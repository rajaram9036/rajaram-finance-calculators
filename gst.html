What is GST Calculator?

GST = Goods and Services Tax. This calculator helps you Add GST to price, Remove GST from MRP, and find GST amount. GST has 4 main slabs in India: 5%, 12%, 18%, 28%.

GST Example: If product price is ₹1000 and GST is 18%, GST Amount = ₹180. Final Price = ₹1180. CGST = ₹90, SGST = ₹90 for intra-state.

GST Rate Slabs in India

GST RateExamples
5%Food grains, Milk, Bread, Tea, Coffee
12%Butter, Mobile Phones, Processed Food
18%Most Goods & Services, AC, Fridge, Restaurants
28%Luxury Cars, Tobacco, AC, Dishwasher

3 Ways to Calculate GST

1

Add GST: When you know base price and want to add GST. Formula: Base + Base x GST%

2

Remove GST: When MRP includes GST and you want base price. Formula: MRP / (1 + GST%)

3

GST Amount: Find how much GST is included in total price.

Key Insight: For intra-state sale CGST + SGST. For inter-state sale IGST. Rate is same.

Disclaimer: GST rates are as per Govt of India. This calculator is for quick estimation only. For billing use professional GST software.
