Salary Breakdown

How is In-Hand Salary Calculated?

In-Hand Salary = CTC - PF - ESI - Professional Tax - Income Tax. This calculator supports both Old and New Tax Regime for FY 2025-26.

Example: CTC ₹12 Lakh. Basic ₹6 Lakh. PF = 12% of Basic = ₹72,000. Tax Old Regime = ₹78,000. In-Hand Monthly = ₹86,500 approx.

Key Deductions from Salary

1

PF: 12% of Basic. Employee + Employer contribution.

2

ESI: 0.75% if salary < ₹21,000/month. For medical benefits.

3

Professional Tax: ₹200 to ₹2500 per year depending on state.

4

Income Tax: As per Old/New regime slabs. Standard deduction ₹50,000.

5

80C Deduction: PF, ELSS, LIC, Home Loan Principal upto ₹1.5 Lakh.

Key Insight: New Regime has lower tax rates but no deductions like 80C, HRA. Old Regime is better if you have home loan + investments.

Disclaimer: Calculation as per FY 2025-26 tax slabs. Actual salary may vary based on company policy. Consult HR for exact breakup.
