What is Education Loan EMI Calculator?

An Education Loan helps students fund higher studies in India or abroad. Interest rate 8.5% to 11%. Tenure 10-15 years. Moratorium period = Course duration + 6-12 months. You pay only simple interest during study period.

Education Loan Example: For ₹10,00,000 loan at 9.5% for 10 years, your EMI = ₹12,901. Total Interest = ₹5,48,120. Total Payment = ₹15,48,120.

5 Benefits of Education Loan

1

Low Interest Rate: 8.5% - 11% is lower than personal loan. Govt subsidized schemes available.

2

Moratorium Period: No EMI during studies. Repayment starts after course + 6-12 months.

3

Tax Benefit u/s 80E: Full interest deduction for 8 years. No upper limit.

4

No Collateral: Up to ₹7.5 Lakh no collateral needed. Above that property/FD needed.

5

Covers All Expenses: Tuition, hostel, books, laptop, travel - everything covered.

Key Insight: Start paying interest during moratorium to reduce total interest. Compare SBI, HDFC Credila, Axis, ICICI for best rates.

Disclaimer: Education Loan rates vary by bank, course and country. Moratorium interest gets added to principal. Tax benefit u/s 80E for 8 years only. This calculator is for illustration only.
